Whose tomorrow might you change today?

We’re committed to using our knowledge and experience to change lives for the better.

Corporate Responsibility

A message from our Chairman and CEO

EY’s purpose – why we exist as an organization – is building a better working world. We strongly believe that by building confidence in the capital markets, helping our clients tackle their toughest issues, running our business according to our values and offering our people exceptional opportunities, we go a long way toward fulfilling that purpose. This forms the core of who we are and what we do daily.

Read on

Changing lives

From one-day interventions to long-term projects, EY people have a wide range of opportunities to make a difference. What unites them is their focus on changing lives. We’re committed to using our knowledge and experience to improve the lives and livelihoods of people in our communities.

Driving inclusive growth

Growth that benefits the few at the expense of the many isn’t inclusive. That’s why we’re using our knowledge and experience to scale innovative, business-based approaches to addressing societal needs. Every day, our people help clients to grow sustainably.

Using that same know-how to help build the capacity and capabilities of high-impact social innovators and job creators creates a ripple effect with the potential to change literally millions of lives.

Using business to change lives

Around the world, social enterprises are innovating new ways to provide people with affordable access to benefits that enhance their quality of life – from clean water and energy to childcare and transportation. Using business to change lives is about helping increase the reach and impact of these business-based approaches, so that many more people can benefit, including in some of the world’s most marginalized communities.

Examples include:

Extending access to safe water in East Africa

Spreading affordable solar energy in Uganda

Expanding access to microfinance in Haiti

Tackling malnutrition in Ethiopia

Exporting social entrepreneurship from Germany

Stimulating inclusive growth and employment

Examples include:

Economies may be growing but, in many places, employment isn’t. Knowing that entrepreneurs are the most consistent engines of job creation, we focus on accelerating development of high-impact entrepreneurs and empowering underrepresented groups to start and grow their own businesses. In this way, we improve opportunities for employment and strengthen the health of local economies.

Supporting the next generation

Young people are three times more likely to be unemployed than adults, yet jobs are out there for those who are properly prepared. As an organization that hires tens of thousands of young people every year, we have great insight into what it takes to succeed in today’s working world. When we apply this insight to education, we help enhance young people’s prospects of finding productive employment.

Helping young people become future-fit

In an age of disruption, what employers want and what young people need most is the ability to continually adapt to change, to recognize opportunities and to innovate in the face of challenges. Helping young people become future-fit is about cultivating those abilities as well as practical skills, through experiential learning about business and entrepreneurship.

Promoting sustainable business

EY is proud to support and advance fundamental principles of sustainable business. From providing a diverse and inclusive place to work, to helping our clients meet the challenge of climate change, to advancing the science and execution of purpose in business, we demonstrate our commitment to building a better world, both inside and outside EY.

Further embedding sustainability inside EY

At EY, we’re constantly working to further integrate principles of inclusiveness, responsibility and sustainability in the way we do business. That includes:

  • Continuing to strengthen our diverse and inclusive culture
  • Making sure that our people continue to live by the highest standards of professional conduct
  • Improving our ability to measure and manage our environmental impact
  • Doing more to encourage high standards in our supply chain

Helping our clients become more sustainable

We’re sharing insights and developing services that offer new ways to make sense of a changing world, to think about the value they create, and to reinvigorate growth and innovation.

Working with others for positive change

Leading with purpose, valuing diversity, driving transparency, creating value for all stakeholders and directing the ingenuity of business towards solving complex social problems – all are at the heart of advancing a more inclusive form of free enterprise and all require long-term and collective effort. That’s why EY is committed to collaborations for positive change.
